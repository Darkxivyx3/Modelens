CubeCon T-Shirt Photoshoot

Client: CubeCon T-Shirt Photoshoot
Project includes: 4 Models, 2 photographers, 1 Makeup artist
Date: June 2024

The Problem

KubeCon needed to photograph their t-shirt designs with the budding college students and sent a box full of cartons for the same.

The Solution

Youthful and Energetic Visuals:
We crafted a photoshoot concept that aligned with KubeCon’s brand aesthetics, focusing on youthful and energetic visuals. The settings and props were chosen to appeal to college students, ensuring that the imagery was relatable and engaging.

Diverse Model Representation:
We selected a diverse group of college-age models to reflect the brand’s inclusivity and to ensure that the target audience could see themselves wearing the t-shirts. This approach helped in creating a connection with the college demographic.

High-Quality Product Shots:
High-resolution images showcased the bags from multiple angles, providing followers with a clear and detailed view of the products. This strategy helped highlight the unique features of each bag.

Interactive and Engaging Content:
To create an immediate impact, we used dynamic and engaging visual elements, including short videos and GIFs, to draw attention and provide a unique browsing experience.
