CubeCon T-Shirt Photoshoot

Modelens Project 3:
Client: CubeCon T-Shirt Photoshoot
Date: June 2024
Project includes: 4 Models, 2 photographers, 1 Makeup artist
Objective: The objective was to create images that resonated with college students, capturing the youthful and dynamic spirit of the brand.

The Problem

KubeCon needed to photograph their t-shirt designs with the budding college students and sent a box full of cartons for the same.

The Solution

Youthful and Energetic Visuals:
We crafted a photoshoot concept that aligned with KubeCon’s brand aesthetics, focusing on youthful and energetic visuals. The settings and props were chosen to appeal to college students, ensuring that the imagery was relatable and engaging.

Diverse Model Representation:
We selected a diverse group of college-age models to reflect the brand’s inclusivity and to ensure that the target audience could see themselves wearing the t-shirts. This approach helped in creating a connection with the college demographic.

High-Quality Product Shots:
High-resolution images showcased the t-shirts from multiple angles, providing clear and detailed views of the designs. This strategy highlighted the unique features of each t-shirt, making them more appealing to potential buyers.

Interactive and Engaging Content:
To create immediate impact, we included dynamic shots and action poses that captured the vibrancy of college life. These engaging visuals were designed to attract attention and provide a unique browsing experience.

The Result

The detailed planning and professional execution allowed us to photograph t-shirt designs in a single session, meeting the project requirements and staying within budget. The high-resolution images were versatile for social media, e-commerce listings, and marketing materials, ensuring a cohesive brand image. The shoot enhanced KubeCon’s brand image, positioning them as vibrant and relatable to college students, while diverse model representation and optimized content broadened audience reach and recognition.
